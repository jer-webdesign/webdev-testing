Artist's Tips:
Whether canvas or textured paper, the surface you paint on affects how your artwork looks. Experiment to find one that enhances the textures and visual effects you want.


Try various brush shapes and sizes to create detailed and expressive art that matches your style. The right brush can bring out emotions and add depth to your work.


Color is more than decoration, it tells stories and evokes feelings. Play with vibrant tones and subtle shades to engage viewers and give your artwork meaning and impact.
